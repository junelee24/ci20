LED LIGHT STRIP MANUAL

STEP 1

CONNECT CABLE TO A POWER SUPPLY THEN CONNECT THE 4 PIN CABLE TO LIGHT STRIP
STEP 2

POSITION THE SENSOR SO THAT IT IS IN SIGHT OF THE REMOTE
STEP 3

TO PROGRAM CUSTOM COLORS, PRESS THE DIY BUTTON OF YOUR CHOICE AND USE THE RGB INCREASE AND DECREASE BUTTONS TO ADJUST THE COLOR.

PRESS THE DIY BUTTON ONCE MORE TO SAVE THE COLOR.



PROBLEMS

1. NOT TURNING ON
2. BRIGHTNESS IS NOT CONSISTENT
SOLUTIONS

1. CHECK POWER OR WIRE CONNECTION
2. INSUFFICIENT POWER SUPPLY, ADD A POWER AMPLIFIER
